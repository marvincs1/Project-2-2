Triumphs Throught the Years...

Liga MX: Club América is the most successful team and the record winners of the competition with thirteen titles. The club’s first-ever win was in 1965–66. Most of the club’s titles were achieved concurrently and it was only once the club defended the league title it won and this was between 1983-84 and 1984-85. In the modern era, América has won up to six titles including wins the Clausura and the Apertura.
