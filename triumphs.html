Triumphs Knowledge

Liga MX Club América: It's the most successful team and the record winners of the competition with thirteen titles. The club’s first-ever win was in 1965–66. Most of the club’s titles were achieved concurrently and it was only once the club defended the league title it won and this was between 1983-84 and 1984-85. In the modern era, América has won up to six titles including wins the Clausura and the Apertura.

The Copa MX is Mexico’s national knockout cup competition. Club América also records Champions of the Copa MX with six titles most of which were achieved in the 20th century. In the modern era of Competition, América most recently won the cup in the 2019 Clausura Tournament.

The Campeón de Campeones is México’s soccer Super Cup played by winners of the Apertura and Clausura. Club América are the second most successful team in the history of the Campeón de Campeones with seven titles behind great national and city rivals Chivas Guadalajara. In 1988, the club won it and successfully defended it the following year in 1989. The most recent win was achieved in 2019. the only domestic Competitions where the club lost the bragging rights as to who is the most successful. In terms of the Liga MX and Copa MX, Club América are ahead of others with several wins ahead.
